Testing your calculator app
===========================

.. case:: Summarize and Clear values in calculator

    .. action:: Type '2+2' into your systems calculator app and press '<enter>'.

        .. reaction:: The calculator sums and shows you '4' as result.

    .. action:: Clear the results, e.g. by typing '<C>' or another button for clearing the results in your app

        .. reaction:: The calculator does show '0' or shows nothing
